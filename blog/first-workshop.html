Empowering the Next Generation: Our First Community Workshop

March 20, 2024 By Cymru Unleashed Team

We're thrilled to share the success of our first community workshop in Cardiff, where we brought together young girls from diverse backgrounds to celebrate Welsh football culture. This workshop marks the beginning of our journey to empower the next generation of Welsh women through sport, art, and digital storytelling.

Workshop Highlights

The day was filled with exciting activities, including:

  • Football skills training with local coaches
  • Art workshops exploring Welsh cultural identity
  • Digital storytelling sessions
  • Team-building exercises

Impact and Feedback

The workshop was attended by 30 girls from various communities across Cardiff. The feedback was overwhelmingly positive, with participants expressing excitement about future events and a newfound connection to Welsh sports culture.

"This workshop showed me that football is for everyone, and I can be proud of my Welsh identity while playing the sport I love." - Workshop Participant

Looking Forward

This successful workshop is just the beginning. We're planning more events across Wales, with the next workshop scheduled for April in Swansea. Stay tuned for more updates and opportunities to get involved!
