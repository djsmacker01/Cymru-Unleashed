Our Legacy

Creating lasting impact beyond UEFA Euro 2025

Legacy Beyond 2025

Cymru Unleashed isn't just a project - it's a movement designed to create lasting change in communities across Wales, with a focus on empowering the next generation of Welsh women through sport, art, and culture.

"From a single summer to a lasting cultural shift - for girls, by girls, in Wales."

Permanent Public Art

A landmark installation in Cardiff Bay Cultural Hub celebrating women in Welsh sport, co-created by young participants and professional artists.

This permanent piece will stand as a visible reminder of the power of women's sport and its cultural significance in Wales.

Digital Archive for Schools

A comprehensive bilingual digital resource featuring VR content, lesson plans, and educational materials on sport, equality, and Welsh culture.

This archive will be freely available to schools across Wales, ensuring the project's impact continues in classrooms for years to come.

Annual Sports-Art Workshops

Through signed MOUs with partner schools, we'll deliver ongoing workshops that blend sports skills with creative expression.

These annual programs will ensure that the connections between sport, identity, and culture continue to be explored by new generations of young Welsh women.

Impact Stories

Through the Cymru Unleashed art installations, I've been able to connect my love of creativity with a newfound appreciation for women's football. The permanent exhibition will feature my work alongside other young Welsh women - something I never thought possible.

Student Participant, Age 16

David Hughes

Community Development Officer, Sport Wales

Supporting our efforts to ensure diversity and inclusion across all legacy programs.

Sport Wales

Providing ongoing support for sports development initiatives in underserved communities.

Arts Council of Wales

Supporting the creation and maintenance of our public art installations and creative programs.

Welsh Government

Ensuring our educational resources align with curriculum requirements and are accessible to all Welsh schools.

Cardiff Council

Supporting our permanent installation in Cardiff Bay and ongoing community events.

Helping deliver our ongoing sports workshops and providing coaching expertise.
