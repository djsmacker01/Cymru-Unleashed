Digital Campaign

Explore our bilingual content and immersive VR experiences

Bilingual Digital Content

Our social media campaign celebrates Welsh women in sport through engaging, shareable content in both English and Welsh, ensuring our message is inclusive and representative of Welsh culture.

  • Dynamic content across Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter
  • Athlete spotlights featuring current and aspiring Welsh sports stars
  • Behind-the-scenes footage from our events and workshops
  • Interactive challenges and participation opportunities
  • Collaboration with Welsh influencers and community leaders
Follow Our Channels

360° VR Journey: "In Her Boots"

Our flagship digital project is an immersive VR experience that follows a young Welsh girl's journey through sport, identity, and culture. Viewers can experience firsthand the challenges, triumphs, and transformative power of sport for young women in Wales.

The VR experience will be available on YouTube VR and showcased at all our events, schools, and community centers. It features bilingual narration and has been created in collaboration with young women from across Wales who shared their stories and perspectives.

Project Timeline

Cymru Unleashed spans from early 2025 through 2026, with activities planned before, during, and after UEFA Women's Euro 2025 to create lasting impact.

Planning & Development

April 2025

Team formation, partnership building, and detailed planning of all activities and events.

VR Content Creation

April - May 2025

Production of our 360° VR experience "In Her Boots" featuring young Welsh women's journeys in sport.

Pre-Tournament Workshops

May - June 2025

First series of sports workshops and art sessions in communities across Wales.

Exhibition Launches

June 2025

Opening of our traveling art exhibitions in Cardiff Bay and other key locations.

Tournament Activities

July 2025

Peak activity during UEFA Women's Euro 2025 with tournaments, viewing parties, and special events.

Digital Campaign Launch

July 2025

Full rollout of social media campaign and VR experience release.

Post-Tournament Events

August - September 2025

Continuing workshops and events to maintain momentum after the tournament.

School Program Integration

September 2025 - 2026

Implementation of ongoing school workshops and educational resources.

Legacy Phase

2026 and beyond

Permanent art installation unveiling, digital archive launch, and ongoing community programs.
